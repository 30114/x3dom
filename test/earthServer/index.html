Climate Modeling and Prediction: 2-D Slicing

Use Case: Climate researchers want to obtain orthogonal 2-D slices from 3-D x/y/t and 4-D x/y/z/t climate simulation output.

The Service: The following Use Cases are being presented, allowing choice of different height values.
Click on cubes to request image slice from server; while the mouse is over the cube, the corresponding WCPS request will be displayed.
